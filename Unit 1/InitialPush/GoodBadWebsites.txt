Good Websites:
  Google- Very simple layout, simple colors, large search bar
  Instagram- Very innovative yet simple, good photos in the background
  Facebook- Simple layout with large typing boxes
  Agar.io- Gets directly into the game, simple, fun layout
  Youtube- Good for someone used to youtube, videos are available with suggestions, etc.

Bad Websites:
  Yahoo- Very jumbled organization, too many pictures, links
  GRT Website- It's not bad for high school website, but text is small, difficult to follow
  Wateronwheels.com- Bright teal background distracts the eyes, too much blank space
  Chestertourist.com- Text is tiny, everything is crammed together
  havenworks.com- too bright and flashy, bad text font, crammed, etc.
